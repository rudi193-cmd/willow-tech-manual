## This manual and the law

Willow is self-hosted software; **your** obligations include privacy law, employment policy, and vendor terms for any cloud keys you attach.

## Warranty coverage (metaphor)

Open-source components carry **their** licenses. Your **internal SLA** is a policy document — link it here when you have one.

## Emissions controls (metaphor)

If you bridge external channels (e.g. messaging gateways), document **retention, logging, and opt-out** the way emissions labels document environmental impact. Silence is not compliance.

## Safety defect reporting

If you discover a **fleet-wide** security defect, follow your coordinated disclosure path (Grove `#architecture` or equivalent) before public post.
