This is the manual a cold engineer reads to become useful without sitting in your channel history. It is not a marketing site. It is not chat-session lore. It is the book you keep in the deployment — clipped to the README, pinned to the binder, slipped into the glove box of the rig.

It comes in three tiers, because the same stack is a different animal depending on who you are today. You build with it, you run it, or you’re just looking. Pick the door that matches.
