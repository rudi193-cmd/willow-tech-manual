Bench rules

The five rules
over the bench.

Taped above every Sticky bench is a hand-printed card. We don’t paint over it; we follow it.

If a chapter says one thing and the card says another, the card wins.

  1. No. 01
    Search the KB before you write. Convergence is the proof; skipping search is how you get two atoms for one fact.
  2. No. 02
    Sandbox stages until you ratify. output/*.json is not live knowledge until a human says so.
  3. No. 03
    Worktrees for non-trivial changes. Branch, build, prove green, then merge to default.
  4. No. 04
    SAFE owns the path. If a script hardcodes a path that bypasses SAFE, it’s a bug ticket, not a shortcut.
  5. No. 05
    The card wins. When chapter and bench disagree, fix the chapter. The bench is the source of truth.
